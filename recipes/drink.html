A sweet and tangy beverage that's perfect for warm days.

Ingredients :

  • 4 cups cubed seedless watermelon
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lime juice (about 4 limes)
  • 2 tablespoons honey or agave syrup (adjust to taste)
  • 2 cups cold water
  • Ice cubes
  • Lime slices and mint leaves for garnish

Instructions :

  1. In a blender, combine the watermelon cubes and lime juice. Blend until smooth.
  2. Add honey or agave syrup and cold water to the blender. Blend again until well mixed.
  3. Taste and adjust sweetness by adding more honey or agave syrup if desired.
  4. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the watermelon mixture over the ice.
  5. Garnish with lime slices and mint leaves.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy!


üçì Strawberry Basil Lemonade

A sweet and herbal twist on the classic lemonade, perfect for warm days.

Ingredients :

  • 1 cup fresh strawberries, hulled and sliced
  • 1/2 cup fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup freshly squeezed lemon juice (about 3-4 lemons)
  • 1/4 cup honey or agave syrup (adjust to taste)
  • 4 cups cold water and Ice Cubes
  • Lemon slices and basil sprigs for garnish

Instructions :

  1. In a blender, combine the strawberries, basil leaves, lemon juice, and honey. Blend until smooth.
  2. Strain the mixture through a fine mesh sieve into a pitcher to remove pulp and seeds.
  3. Add cold water to the pitcher and stir to combine.
  4. Taste and adjust sweetness by adding more honey or agave syrup if desired.
  5. Fill glasses with ice cubes and pour the strawberry basil lemonade over the ice.
  6. Garnish with lemon slices and basil sprigs.
  7. Serve immediately and enjoy!
