Eggless Nutella brownies

Brownies are rich, chocolatey squares of pure comfort. With their fudgy centers, crackly tops, and intense cocoa flavor, they're the perfect blend of cake and candy. Whether plain, nut-studded, or swirled with Nutella, a good brownie is a timeless treat that satisfies every sweet craving.
